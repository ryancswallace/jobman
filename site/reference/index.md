compatibility rule.

  generated from the production Cobra command tree.
- The [configuration schema]({{ site.baseurl }}/reference/configuration/)

- [Selectors, output, and exit status]({{ site.baseurl }}/reference/output/)
  records the stable automation surface.
- [Platform support]({{ site.baseurl }}/reference/platforms/) explains native
  process, input, storage, and evidence differences.
- The [compatibility contract]({{ site.baseurl }}/reference/compatibility/)
